About Leksiyonity

Leksiyonity is designed to revolutionize the learning experience by integrating education with gamification, socialization, and organization. Our goal is to create a stress-free and engaging environment for all students.

Problem Statement

Overloaded Curriculum

Minimal breaks and poor pacing in academic schedules.

Lack of Mental Health Support

Insufficient mental health support groups in universities.

Inaccessible Services

High cost of tutoring, productivity apps, and wellness services.

Disorganization & Stress

Dependence on multiple applications leads to disorganization and increased stress.

Our Solution

To create a digital gaming application that is integrated with studying, socializing and organizing all in one to create a stress-free school environment for all students.
