OUR SUBSCRIPTION PLANS

Choose the plan that's right for you!

Free Plan

₱0/month

Target:

Students or users exploring the app

Features:

  • Access to basic learning modules (e.g., first 2 levels/lessons per subject)
  • Daily login rewards
  • Ads included
  • Limited quizzes per day (e.g., 3 quizzes/day)
  • Community leaderboard (limited access)

Goal:

Attract users and let them experience the value before upgrading.

Starter Plan

₱199/month

Target:

Entry-level learners who want more than the basics

Features:

  • No ads
  • Full access to all learning levels for 2 subjects
  • Unlock daily missions and challenges
  • 1x Weekly performance report
  • Access to limited customization (e.g., avatars/themes)

Annually Cost:

₱1,915 (₱160/mo) save - 20%

Plus Plan

₱349/month

Target:

Regular users or students aiming for better learning outcomes

Features:

  • Everything in Starter Plan
  • Access to all subjects
  • Personalized learning paths
  • Parental dashboard access (progress, scores, weak areas)
  • Daily challenges with bonus XP
  • Monthly leaderboard badges
  • Offline access to lessons

Annually Cost:

₱3,345 (₱279/mo)

Pro Plan

₱599/month

Target:

Dedicated learners, or educators who want the full experience

Features:

  • Everything in Plus Plan
  • Live tutorial sessions (1x/month or group-based)
  • Weekly personalized reports with insights
  • Early access to new content
  • Priority support
  • Gamified collaboration – join or create a learning team
  • Advanced customization (themes, avatars, music, power-ups)

Annually Cost:

₱5,750 (₱479/mo)
